faq

What is Mixo?

Mixo is the ultimate DJ library management tool that helps you sync, organise, and export your music across all your devices. Mixo gives you full control over your playlists, tracks, and metadata, ensuring everything is ready for your next set.

How much does Mixo cost?

Mixo offers a 7-day free trial, after which you can choose between our Free plan or upgrade to Mixo Gold for $7/month, unlocking additional features.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel your Mixo subscription anytime through our self-service portal—no hassle, no stress. Simply log in, manage your account, and cancel your plan when needed.

Where can I use Mixo?

Mixo works on macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS, and Android devices. While an internet connection is required to access your music stored in cloud services like Dropbox or Google Drive, you can also sync your music to your device for offline use.

What can I do with Mixo?

  • Sync your library across multiple devices
  • Transfer metadata between DJ software platforms like Rekordbox, Serato, and Traktor
  • Organize playlists, cue points, ID3 tags, and more
  • Manage duplicate tracks with the Duplicate Checker
  • Use Key Conversion and Find/Replace tools for fast library adjustments
  • Listen to music offline with the mobile app
  • And much more!

Where can I get help?

If you need help or have any questions, visit our Help Center at http://mixo.dj/support. We also provide detailed articles and guides to help you get the most out of Mixo.

What is the difference between the free and Gold plans?

Our free plan allows you to import and organise your DJ library. Mixo Gold ($7/month) offers additional features including:

  • Sync across multiple DJ software platforms
  • Sync to a second computer and mobile devices
  • Download playlists and listen offline
  • Duplicate Checker and advanced Find/Replace tools
  • Key Conversion and cue point management tools

How many devices can use Mixo at once?

You can sync Mixo across 2 computers and use the mobile app for on-the-go access. This means you can have your library accessible on both your desktop setup and your mobile devices, keeping your DJ tools handy wherever you are.
